THE AKAN

The Akan Culture & Naming
The Akan are a meta-ethnicity living in the countries of the present day Ghana and Ivory Coast in West Africa.
The Akan language also known as Twi/Fate are a group of dialects within the central Tano branch of the Potou-Tan
subfamily of the Niger Congo family The Akan people of Ghana frequently name their children after the day of the week they were born These “day names” have further meanings concerning the soul and characterof the person. Middle names have considerably more variety and can refer to their birth order, twin status or an ancestors middle name
